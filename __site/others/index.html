My past career

I was a professional ballet dancer with Ballet West and Zurich Ballet. I have extensive knowledge of the culture, career paths, artistry, and athleticism involved in both classical ballet and contemporary dance. My artistic view is strongly influenced by Igal Perry, artistic director of Peridance Center. If you or your children are interested in ballet, feel free to reach out to me.
